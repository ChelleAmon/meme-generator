How to make a meme!


  1. Upload an image

    2. Click on the "Choose File" button to upload your image of choice and get Memeing!

  2. Add some text.

    Add your captions on the text bar beside the canvas. Add a top and/or bottom caption.

  3. Generate Meme Click on the button below the canvas "Generate Meme"

  4. Save and Share To save, right click your mouse on the canvas and save in what ever format you'd like and start Sharing with your friends!
